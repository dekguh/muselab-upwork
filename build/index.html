What is the
MUSE DAO?

DAO stands for "Decentralized Autonomous Organization". The MUSE DAO focuses on building different decentralized applications to further the NFT space. Anyone can join the DAO by having enough support by it’s members.

How does the DAO work?

The community can propose and vote on updates to the different protocols that get build, or builders can create proposals to get funding and build for the MUSE DAO, the benefit of building for the MUSE DAO is an existing community, support from our existing developers and designers, shared experience and $funds.

How is the MUSE token helpful for the MUSE DAO?

MUSE is the governance token of the dao, with $MUSE you can vote to decide the future of the DAO and what gets built, read here on the story of how muse came about (link to a future page)
